## Why a separate license

The integration source code (under `custom_components/vag_connect/`,
`scripts/`, `tests/`) is licensed under Apache 2.0 — permissive,
HACS-compatible, fine for commercial use.

Research artifacts (architectural analysis, reverse-engineering
notes, atlas extraction data, strategic decision documents) represent
substantial investigative work and carry a stricter license to
prevent uncredited commercial appropriation.

If your use case requires more than CC BY-NC-ND allows, contact the
author for a separate arrangement.
